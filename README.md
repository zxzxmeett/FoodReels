FOODREELS is a short-video food discovery platform inspired by reels-style content.  
Users can explore food reels, interact with them, and food partners can upload and manage their own food content.

---

## Features

### Normal Users
- View food reels
- Like food reels
- Save food reels
- Browse food content seamlessly

### Food Partners
- Upload food reels
- Manage uploaded reels
- Have a dedicated profile page
- Promote food items through reels

---
